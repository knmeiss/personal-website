Keeping Up Without Burning Out: My AI Workflow for DevRel

DevRelCon NYC • Industry City, Brooklyn, NY

The pace of AI development feels overwhelming. New tools, techniques, and paradigms emerge weekly. As someone driving AI adoption at Amazon while trying to stay current myself, I've developed a personal workflow and mindset that helps me keep up without burning out. In this lightning talk, I'll share my current toolbox, the questions I ask myself that help me level up, and how I decide when to use AI vs. do things myself to actually learn. This isn't about having all the answers. It's about building a sustainable approach to working in a field that moves faster than any of us can follow.
